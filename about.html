My professional experience

I started to work as a graphic designer when I finished the German higher education entrance qualification for about 10 years and worked in different places, as I am always interested in new things and in learning new skills to build a great work experience. In 2020 I felt like it was time for new experiences and to turn my knowledge and skills into something which felt more valuable to me. So I got certified as a User Experience Designer, as I always loved concepts and focusing on the person who interacts with design at the end. Additional I got certified as a Customer Analyst, which includes skills in Marketing, Sales Management and Leadership, to evolve my sense for the customers needs from more points of view.
